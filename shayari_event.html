Lafzon ka Karvan: A Shayari, Poetry And Jugalbandi Event

The expression of our emotions needs rhythm to sound vibrant to our audience and the rhythm is brought through a poetic accent that can be molded in the form of Shayari when the alfaaz of Urdu are embedded gracefully and conveyed with nazakat. We "Sanskriti"- the cultural committee of MIET are going to organize an event to show-cast your poetic side and strive to astonish everyone with your artistry vision of words and rhythm.

Poetry is an art; sometimes a lesson learnt or sometimes a philosophy, sometimes it is an outbrust of emotions or a deep thought. It a literary composition which envokes a concentrated imaginative awareness of experience or a specific emotional response

We at Sanskriti celebrate our love for Poetry in form of fiesta which is filled with skillful harmony of meaning, sound and rhythm. So, get ready to indulge in your poetic side and pour out your feelings in form of words.
