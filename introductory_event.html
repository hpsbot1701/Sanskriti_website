AAGAAZ - Sanskriti Cultural Event

Infusing life into lifeless objects

Event Description

It's never easy to infuse life into lifeless world objects, but a touch from great artists can bring any inanimate object to life. Comics is an excellent combination of both art and literature which uses this magical touch to depict narratives in a manner that appeals instantly to any reader's heart and mind.

From manga fans to fantasy admirers, everyone loves comics and puppets. For those of you who like to live in a reverie world, Sanskriti the cultural committee of the college with its first event for this academic year offers you the opportunity to display the talent chirping within you by turning your imagination into reality. So, levitate your ideas and bring magic into ordinary paper sheets and objects.

Sneak Peek

  • COMICON

    Create your own comic strip using your imagination and storytelling skills. Let your characters come alive on paper.

  • HANDY MANDY

    Create your own puppet using waste material and turn it into a work of art.

UDBHAV - Sanskriti Cultural Event

Infusing life into lifeless objects

Event Description

Since the dawn of time, mankind has been an aesthete. True art is channelled through the loving heart, guided by emotions that stir the soul of loving bonds and the sort of imagination that is free and childish in its sense of wonder and joy. The difference between painting a sun as a yellow spot and painting a yellow spot as the sun is -ART.

Polish your artistic skills, accelerate the pedals of your mind and get ready to spill your masterpiece from your imagination. We "Sanskriti"- the cultural committee gives everyone the opportunity to dive in the astounding beauty of our art and culture and embrace this event as the pleasant smell of the first rain.

Sneak Peek

  • Riddlogamy

    In this game, players take turns drawing a scene or scenario that represents a riddle or a set of riddles, and other players must guess what the riddle(s) represent based on the drawing. The game can be played in teams or individually, and the player or team with the most correct answers at the end of the game wins.
