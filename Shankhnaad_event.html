Shankhnaad Event

Shankhnaad, organized by the Sanskriti Committee, is the biggest event of MIET College. It is a three-day event that showcases the talent of the students in various areas of performing arts. The event is composed of three separate events that take place on different days:

  • Echoes
  • Curtain Raiser
  • Hop til you drop

Echoes is a singing event that allows students to showcase their vocal talents. Curtain Raiser is a skit and drama event that gives students the opportunity to express themselves through acting and stage performance. Hop til you drop is a dancing event that allows students to showcase their dancing skills and styles.

Kolahaal is the biggest fest of MIET College that is organized after the Shankhnaad event. It is also a three-day event that brings together students from various colleges to participate in cultural activities and competitions.

One unique feature of Kolahaal is that the winners of Shankhnaad get a direct chance to perform on the Kolahaal stage without any auditions. This is a great opportunity for the students to showcase their talents on a larger stage and receive recognition for their hard work.
