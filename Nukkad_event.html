Nukkad Natak Event organized by Sanskriti Committee

Stop Wasting Food

The Sanskriti Committee in MIET College organized a Nukkad Natak event to raise awareness about the issue of food waste. The skit performed by the students showcased how much food is wasted in households, events, and restaurants. They highlighted the negative impact of food waste on our environment, society, and economy. The skit emphasized the importance of reducing food waste by being mindful of portion sizes, storing food properly, and donating excess food to those in need. The audience was also informed about how food waste contributes to climate change and that reducing food waste can save money and resources.

Corona Virus

The Nukkad Natak event organized by the Sanskriti Committee in MIET College also focused on spreading awareness about the Corona Virus. The students performed a skit that emphasized the importance of following COVID-19 protocols such as wearing masks, maintaining social distance, and washing hands regularly. They also highlighted the importance of getting vaccinated to protect ourselves and others from the virus. The skit conveyed the message that every individual has a role to play in stopping the spread of the virus and that it is our responsibility to keep ourselves and our community safe.

Say No to Plastic

The third skit performed by the Sanskriti Committee in MIET College was focused on the issue of plastic. The students showcased how plastic is harmful to the environment and how it affects wildlife and marine life. They also highlighted the fact that plastic takes hundreds of years to decompose and that it is essential to reduce our usage of plastic to save the planet. The skit emphasized the importance of using alternatives to plastic like cloth bags, paper bags, and reusable bottles. The audience was encouraged to make small changes in their daily lives to reduce their usage of plastic.

It is worth mentioning that only members of the Sanskriti Committee participated in the Nukkad Natak event. They put in a lot of effort to make the event a success and were able to engage and educate the audience effectively through their performances. Overall, the event was a great success in raising awareness about important social and environmental issues.
