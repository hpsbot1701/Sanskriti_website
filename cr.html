Curtain Raiser

Acting is an art form that requires a strong sense of imagination, emotional sensitivity, physiological self-expression, vocal clarity and dramatic expression skills. The Curtain Raiser event is an opportunity for actors to showcase their skills and talents. It's a platform to tell a story by adopting the personality and life of a character.

With any part you play, there is a certain amount of yourself in it. So, expand your imagination, strengthen your body and voice, boost your expression and get a rush of adrenaline as curtains are about to raise.
