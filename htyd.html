Hop Till You Drop

About Event

Dance is one of the purest forms of self-expression, with unfiltered emotions flowing through the performer's bodies straight into the consciousness of the audience. It is like dreaming with your feet. We "Sanskriti" the cultural committee presents "Hop till you drop" a worth taking opportunity to express your dancing instinct from solo to group and from classical to western dance form. Let your personality shine through your moves. Put your heart and soul into your dance and share your creativity with us.
