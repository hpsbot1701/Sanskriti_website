Echos - A Mystical Musical Event

About Echoes

"Echos" is an inaugural event of "Shankhnaad" organized by "Sanskriti" - the cultural committee. It is a mystical musical event where you can showcase your vocal talent through a variety of music genres, from acapella to instrumental, bollywood to crescendo.

Echos encourages you to get on the floor, compete with the elite and tell your story through your spellbinding harmonies. So if you have a passion for music, come and join us for an unforgettable experience.
